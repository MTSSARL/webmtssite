Our Expertise

Comprehensive Industrial Automation Solutions

Special Machines

Design, manufacturing and integration of custom special machines for automated assembly, testing, and production processes across automotive and industrial sectors.

Machine Vision

Defect detection, dimensional control, barcode reading, deep learning-based intelligent inspection solutions for quality assurance in manufacturing.

Robotics

Pick and place, depalletizing and palletizing, decasing and encasing, robotic gluing and dispensing, end-of-arm tooling (EOAT) for automated production.

Conveyor Systems

Indexed pallet conveyors, chain, belt and band conveyors, carousel conveyors for automated material handling and production line feeding.

Electrical Engineering

Design, manufacturing and integration of electrical equipment: cable harnesses, cabinets, control panels for industrial applications.

MTS primarily serves the automotive industry and its tier-1 suppliers, including manufacturers of connectors, electronics, filtration systems, and wire harnesses. We also serve general manufacturing and industrial sectors.

MTS is headquartered in Tanger, Morocco at Z.A.E Bni Ouassine, Lot 58. We serve clients across Morocco and internationally with on-site installation and support capabilities.
