Welcome to MTS

Your Trusted Automation Partner in Morocco

MTS is a manufacturing system technology company specializing in the design, manufacturing and integration of special machines, machine vision, conveyor systems, robotics, and electrical engineering solutions. With a strong presence across multiple production sites in Morocco, we serve tier-1 automotive clients including Marelli, APTIV, TE Connectivity, and OPmobility.
